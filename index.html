About Us

The Geek Shop is a Bangladesh-based, community-driven store run by a small group of tech and mechanical keyboard enthusiasts. Our goal is simple: to make quality keyboards, keycaps, switches, and accessories more accessible to the local enthusiast community. We mainly operate through a pre-order system, which helps us bring in unique and premium products at fair prices. In some cases, we also offer limited ready-stock items based on demand. Everything we do is focused on authenticity, transparency, and building a strong geek community in Bangladesh.

FAQs

Our pre-order system allows you to reserve products before they arrive in stock. You pay a portion upfront (typically 25%), and the rest upon delivery. This helps us import premium items based on demand.

Delivery within Dhaka typically takes 2-3 days, while outside Dhaka may take 4-7 days. Pre-order items may take longer depending on import timelines.

We accept Bkash for full or partial payments and Cash on Delivery (COD) with an advance delivery fee.

Payment Options

  • Cash on Delivery (With Advance Delivery Fee)
